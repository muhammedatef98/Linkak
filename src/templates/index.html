Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Linkak different from other link-in-bio tools?

Linkak offers professional branding features, advanced analytics, custom domain URL shortening, and a business menu builder that other platforms don't provide. We focus on helping professionals and businesses enhance their online presence.

Can I use my own domain with Linkak?

Yes! You can connect your own domain to your Linkak profile and use it for URL shortening with our Pro and Business plans.

How does the business menu builder work?

Our menu builder allows you to create digital menus with categories, items, prices, and descriptions. You can add these menus to your Linkak profile or share them directly with a unique link or QR code.

What analytics does Linkak provide?

Linkak provides detailed visitor demographics, geographic data, device information, click-through rates, conversion tracking, and heat maps to help you optimize your profile performance.
